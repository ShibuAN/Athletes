Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Athletes ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you connect your Strava account to our platform.

2. Information We Collect

When you authorize our application via Strava, we collect the following data:

  • Your Strava Athlete ID and name.
  • Your workout activities (distance, duration, type, and date).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use this data solely to:

  • Display your own activity history on your private dashboard.
  • Include your stats on the platform leaderboard.

Note: Per Strava's 2026 guidelines, your detailed activity data is only displayed to you. We do not use your data for AI training or share it with third parties.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures (Supabase) to protect your authentication tokens and activity data.

5. Your Rights & Deletion

You can disconnect your Strava account at any time through your settings. To request full deletion of your data from our servers, please contact us or delete your profile within the app.
